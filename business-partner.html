Partner Agreement

ParkEase Business Partner Agreement

1. Revenue Sharing: ParkEase will retain 40% of all booking revenue generated through the platform. The remaining 60% will be transferred to the partner's registered bank account every 7 days.

2. Platform Responsibilities: ParkEase will provide the booking platform, payment processing, customer support, and marketing services. Partners are responsible for maintaining the parking space as described.

3. Partner Responsibilities: Partners must maintain the agreed number of parking slots, ensure security and safety of vehicles, and adhere to the described operating hours. Any changes must be communicated 48 hours in advance.

4. Cancellation Policy: Bookings cancelled by the customer 2+ hours in advance receive a full refund. Bookings cancelled within 2 hours are non-refundable. Partners cannot cancel accepted bookings without penalty.

5. Quality Standards: Partners must maintain a minimum 3.5/5 star rating. Ratings below this threshold will result in a review and potential suspension from the platform.

6. Payment Processing: All payments are processed via Razorpay. Partners will receive payments after deducting the platform commission and applicable taxes.

7. Termination: Either party may terminate this agreement with 30 days written notice. ParkEase reserves the right to immediately suspend partners who violate terms.

  • Partners agree to provide accurate information about their space
  • Partners consent to background verification of the property
  • Partners agree to GST compliance for their earnings
  • Partners understand the 40/60 revenue split is non-negotiable for the standard plan
