Frequently Asked Questions

How do I cancel a booking?

You can cancel your booking up to 2 hours before your check-in time via the booking confirmation email or by calling our support line.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm), credit/debit cards, net banking, and cash at the facility.

Is my vehicle safe at ParkEase?

Yes! All our facilities have 24/7 CCTV surveillance, security personnel, and covered parking options.

Can I extend my parking time?

Yes, you can extend your slot duration via the app or by calling support at least 30 minutes before your check-out time.
