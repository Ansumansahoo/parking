Frequently Asked Questions

How do I cancel a booking?

You can cancel your booking free of charge up to 2 hours before your check-in time. Contact support or use your booking reference to cancel.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept all major debit/credit cards, UPI, net banking, and popular wallets like Paytm and PhonePe via Razorpay.

Can I extend my parking time?

Yes! You can extend your booking by calling our 24/7 support line or by visiting the parking attendant on-site.

How do I list my space as a partner?

Visit our Partner With Us page to register your parking space and start earning 60% of every booking.
