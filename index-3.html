Pricing FAQ

Is there a free trial?
Yes! Sign up for free and get your first day of Basic parking at no charge. No credit card required to start.
Can I switch plans anytime?
Absolutely. You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect from the next billing cycle.
What payment methods are accepted?
We accept all major debit/credit cards, UPI, net banking, Paytm, PhonePe, and other popular wallets through our secure Razorpay payment gateway.
Are there any hidden charges?
No hidden charges whatsoever. The price you see is the price you pay. GST (18%) is included in the displayed price.
What is the cancellation policy?
Free cancellation up to 2 hours before your booking start time. Cancellations after that may be subject to a 50% charge.
